Altekma Group

We understand the importance of innovation and professionalism

Altekma maintains its leadership in the industry for 21 years with its quality products, customer satisfaction and after-sales service understanding, and has been indexing its target to sustainable growth and development by raising the success rate each year. Altekma produces sandblasting boilers, texturing machines, water jets, road marking trucks and various spare parts.

At the same time, these products are exported to Europe, The Middle East, North Africa, Turki Republics and Altekma is getting stronger every day in this area.
